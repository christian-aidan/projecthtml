            <h2>Ingredients:</h2>
            <ul>
                <li>2 cups all-purpose flour</li>
                <li>1/4 cup vegetable oil</li>
                <li>1/2 teaspoon salt</li>
                <li>1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds</li>
                <li>1/2 teaspoon coriander powder</li>
                <li>1/2 teaspoon garam masala</li>
                <li>1 cup boiled and minced meat (chicken, beef, or lamb)</li>
                <li>1/2 cup boiled peas</li>
                <li>Oil for frying</li>
                <li>Salt to taste</li>
            </ul>
        </div>

        <div class="instructions">
            <h2>Instructions:</h2>
            <ol>
                <li>In a bowl, mix flour, oil, salt, and water to make a smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.</li>
                <li>Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds, coriander powder, and garam masala. Cook for a minute.</li>
                <li>Add minced meat, boiled peas, and salt. Mix well and cook until the meat is fully cooked. Remove from heat and let it cool.</li>
                <li>Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a thin oval-shaped sheet.</li>
                <li>Cut the sheet in half to form two semi-circles. Fold each semi-circle into a cone and stuff it with the meat filling.</li>
                <li>Seal the edges of the samosas by pressing them gently.</li>
                <li>Heat oil in a deep pan. Fry the samosas until they turn golden brown and crispy.</li>
                <li>Remove from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.</li>
                <li>Serve hot with mint chutney or tamarind sauce.</li>
            </ol>
